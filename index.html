How We Can Help

Our Amazing Services

CircutUp has experienced engineering to facilitate a variety of customer requirements. Including PCB design services, Firmware Development, Standalone software, Web Application, and Manufacturing services.

PCB DESIGN

With a layer capability of 1 to 8 layers, we specialize in crafting Rigid PCBs, Flexible PCBs, and Aluminum PCBs to suit your unique requirements. Our expertise spans across a wide range of applications, including IoT, Power Electronics, Wireless devices, PDAs, High-Speed electronics, and more. Utilizing industry-leading CAD software like Altium and KiCAD, our experienced team ensures precise and reliable PCB designs that propel your innovations to new heights.

FIRMWARE DEVELOPMENT

Our experienced team excels in firmware development using industry-standard languages like C/C++. We have a proven track record of expertise in various platforms, including STM32, MSP430, ESP-IDF, and Arduino. Whether you're working on intricate IoT solutions, embedded systems, or cutting-edge microcontroller projects, we have the know-how to deliver top-notch firmware solutions tailored to your specific requirements.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

CircuitUp’s Web team is proficient in a range of languages, including PHP, JavaScript, and Python, allowing us to adapt to your specific project requirements. Leveraging the power of popular frameworks like Laravel and Node.js, we design and develop web dashboards that deliver seamless functionality and exceptional performance. Our expertise extends to integrating AWS services, implementing API functionalities.

DESKTOP APPLICATION

We are also specializes in creating user-friendly applications that support Windows OS. Leveraging the power of C#, we design versatile configurator software for your electronic devices, providing seamless control and customization options. Additionally, we excel in developing tailored desktop applications to meet specific client use cases, ensuring your unique requirements are met with precision. Whether it's streamlining processes, enhancing user experiences, or driving productivity.

MANUFACTURING

At CircuitUp, we go above and beyond to ensure a seamless electronic development experience for our clients. In addition to our core services, we also offer comprehensive electronic manufacturing solutions. From procurement service to box assembling, firmware loading, end product testing, and validating, we take care of every step of the process. Our expertise also extends to enclosure designing, ensuring that your electronic products are ready for market with precision.
